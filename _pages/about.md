Hi!

My name is Paul Binder, I am a docotoral candidate in Political Science and a Research Associate in the project PINPOINT, located at the Geschwister Scholl Institute of Political Science at LMU Munich, where I work under the supervision of Professor Timm Betz. 

My research focuses on the intersection of (international) political economy and conflict studies. 

Before starting my current position, I completed an M.A. and B.A. (with a minor in Economics) in Political Science at LMU Munich.
